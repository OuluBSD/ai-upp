# Task: OTP Extraction & Display
# Status: PENDING

## Objective
Detect and provide easy access to One-Time Passwords (OTPs) from incoming SMS.

## Requirements
- **Detection**: Use regex/heuristics to identify OTP codes in the latest messages.
- **UI Element**: A dedicated sidebar or top banner for "Latest OTP".
- **Action**: "Copy to Clipboard" button next to the detected code.
- **Automation**: Option to automatically copy new OTPs to the clipboard if the app is focused.
