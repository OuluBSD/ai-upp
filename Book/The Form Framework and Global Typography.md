# The Form Framework and Global Typography
**Date Span:** 2010-05-01 to 2010-05-31

### Introduction of the Form Framework
Launched the **Form** core package along with a dedicated editor. This provided a high-level, declarative approach to building data-entry interfaces, streamlining the development of complex business applications.

### Typographical and CJK Refinements
Undertook a deep refactor of font metrics and character replacement logic. Addressed "missing glyph" issues for CJK text and introduced the `FontCover` utility to analyze system font coverage across Win32 and X11.

### Scatter and Bazaar Expansion
`Scatter` gained significant performance boots with `SetSequentialX` and `SetFastViewX`. The Bazaar saw the addition of SHA2 functions to `AESStream`, ActiveX support in `Controls4U`, and high-performance 5-argument callbacks in `MtAlt`.

### Professional Tooling and Globalization
TheIDE's PDB debugger was upgraded with better array expression support. The database layer was hardened with `WhenReconnect` for PGSQL and improved error handling for Oracle. The framework's global footprint peak with exhaustive website translations into multiple languages.
