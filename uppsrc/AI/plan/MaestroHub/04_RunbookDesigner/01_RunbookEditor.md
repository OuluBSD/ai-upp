# Task: Visual Runbook Editor
# Status: TODO

## Objective
GUI for creating and editing Runbooks (`maestro runbook add/edit`).

## Requirements
- **Metadata Form**: Title, Scope, Tags, Status.
- **Step List**: Drag-and-drop reordering of steps.
- **Step Editor**: Form for Actor, Action, Expected Result, Command, Details.
- **Resolve Dialog**: Input text area to call `RunbookManager::Resolve` (AI generation).
- **Preview**: Live preview of the Markdown/PUML output.
